About Shanti Education Research Foundation

Shanti Education Research Foundation has now members in Slovakia, Estonia, Romania, Ukraine, USA, Poland, Nigeria, Tanzania, Georgia, Australia, and Africa, boosting networking opportunities that strengthen ties within and across countries and communities that are technical.

Shanti Education Research Foundation members are playing a dynamic role in being on the organizing committee, Editorial Board, Reviewers committee, and Advisory Committee of International conventions & Scientific events.
